🎓 What is this simulator?

A tool for interviewing historical figures. You ask questions; the figure answers from within their own historical moment and worldview.

כלי לתרגול ראיונות עם דמויות היסטוריות. שאל/י שאלות וקבל/י תשובות מנקודת המבט של הדמות.

🔄 Three phases / שלושה שלבים

Phase 1 (turns 1–10): You interview the figure. They answer from within their historical reality — no hindsight, no modern judgement. From around turn 7 the tone begins to sharpen.

Phase 2 (turns 11–20): The figure turns the tables. Still in character, now aware it's 2026, it challenges your reasoning — not by insult, but by pressing you to defend the line you draw between yourself and it.

Debrief: The simulation ends and a facilitator (not the figure) reflects with you on what happened, Socratically. It begins automatically after turn 20, or whenever you press "End & reflect" / type /debrief.

👤 How to start / איך מתחילים

Type the name of the figure you want to interview — specific (e.g. "Hitler", "Stalin", "Ben Gurion") or general (e.g. "A Roman soldier").

הקלד/י את שם הדמות — ספציפית (למשל "היטלר", "בן גוריון") או כללית (למשל "חייל רומי").

👥 Multiple figures / כמה דמויות

Up to 4 figures simultaneously — they will respond to each other as well as to you.

💾 Saving / שמירה

The conversation is saved automatically in your browser and restored when you reopen.

🔚 Ending / סיום

The simulation moves to a reflective debrief automatically after turn 20. You can also end early at any time with the "End & reflect" button or by typing /debrief. Use Export transcript to save the full conversation.
